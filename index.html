The problem isn’t “choosing a place”. It’s losing trust mid-way.

The Problem

You scroll for 20 minutes. You compare options. You finally find “the one”. Then at checkout the price jumps: cleaning fee, service fee, taxes.

Why it matters

That moment changes everything. You start doubting your choice. Sometimes you leave and start over. Booking should feel simple — instead it feels stressful.

The Solution

Transparent Stay shows the total price from the beginning. What you see is what you actually pay. No hidden add-ons later. Just clarity.
